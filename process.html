Step-by-Step Guidance Process

Our transparent 7-step process ensures you're fully prepared for your study abroad journey. Each step builds upon the previous one to create a solid foundation.

1
Week 1

Initial Consultation & Profile Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your academic background, career goals, and study preferences.

Academic record evaluation and gap analysis
Career goal alignment and realistic expectation setting
Financial capacity assessment and budget planning
Outcome

Clear understanding of your profile and realistic study abroad options for Japan or UK.

2
Week 2-3

Country & Program Selection

Based on your profile, we help you choose between Japan and UK, and select appropriate programs that match your goals.

Detailed comparison of Japan vs UK education systems
Institution and program recommendation based on eligibility
Intake selection (April/September for Japan, September/January for UK)
Outcome

Finalized country selection and shortlisted 3-5 suitable institutions.

3
Month 1-6 (Japan) / Month 1-2 (UK)

Language Preparation (Japan Track Only)

For Japan-bound students, we provide intensive Japanese language training (N5/N4) in our offline classrooms.

Structured offline classroom training (3 hours daily, 6 days/week)
JLPT/NAT test preparation and practice exams
Conversational practice and cultural orientation
Outcome

Achieve required Japanese proficiency (N5 minimum) for application and daily life in Japan.

4
Month 2-3

Application Preparation & Submission

We guide you through the complete application process, ensuring all documents meet institutional requirements.

Document checklist and preparation guidance
SOP/Personal statement review and improvement
Application form completion and submission monitoring
Outcome

Complete application package submitted to selected institutions.

5
Month 3-4

Admission & Offer Letter Processing

We monitor your application status and help you understand and respond to admission offers.

Regular follow-up with institutions on application status
Offer letter review and explanation of terms and conditions
Acceptance letter preparation and submission
Outcome

Secured admission offer from at least one institution and acceptance confirmation.

6
Month 4-5

Visa Application & Interview Preparation

We guide you through the visa application process and prepare you for the credibility interview.

Complete visa document checklist and preparation
Financial document verification and arrangement guidance
Mock interviews and question-answer preparation sessions
Outcome

Complete visa application submitted and interview-ready preparation.

7
Month 5-6

Pre-Departure Orientation & Travel Arrangements

Final preparation covering accommodation, travel, cultural adjustment, and what to expect in your destination country.

Accommodation arrangement guidance (dormitory/homestay/apartment)
Flight booking assistance and airport pickup information
Cultural orientation and country-specific survival guide
Outcome

Fully prepared for departure with all necessary arrangements completed.

General Requirements & Documents

While specific requirements vary by country and institution, here are the common documents needed for a successful application.

Academic Documents

  • All academic certificates and transcripts
  • Mark sheets from SSC/HSC to Bachelor's
  • Medium of instruction certificate (if available)
  • Transcript conversion to GPA (if required)
Important

All documents must be attested by the Education Board and Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Language Proficiency

  • For Japan: JLPT/NAT Test Certificate (N5 minimum)
  • For UK: IELTS/TOEFL/PTE Academic certificate
  • Test score validity (usually 2 years)
  • Test center original score report
Important

Some UK universities accept medium of instruction certificate instead of IELTS.

Financial Documents

  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Bank solvency certificate
  • Sponsor's financial documents
  • Income tax documents (if applicable)
Important

Funds must be in liquid form and properly documented. Loan sanction letters are also acceptable.

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Birth certificate
  • NID/Passport copies of parents/sponsors
  • Passport-size photographs (recent)
Important

All documents must be in English or officially translated. Keep multiple copies of everything.
